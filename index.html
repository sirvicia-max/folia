📌 What is a Mark?

A mark is anything on the web worth remembering — an article, a tool, a course, an ad you noticed. Folia is your personal research board.

✦ Saving a Mark

1
Click "Add Mark"
Or press ⌘N / Ctrl+N from anywhere.
2
Choose Auto-fetch or Manual
Auto-fetch grabs the title and cover image automatically from the URL. Manual lets you fill everything in yourself.
3
Write a note
Capture why you saved it. This is the most valuable field.
4
Set a status
Mark it as To visit, In progress, or Done.
💡 Tip: Hover any card and click the pencil on the image to change its cover without opening the full edit form.

🔄 Progress Status

To visit Saved but not opened yet
In progress You've started
Done Finished or no longer needed
💡 Click the status badge on any card to cycle through states without opening the editor.
